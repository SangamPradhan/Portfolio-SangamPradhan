Gratifying Spa & Parlor — Website

gratifyingspa.com.np is a mobile-first, performance-optimized spa & parlor website built with Laravel during a paid internship. The site focuses on a fast, accessible booking experience, product/voucher ordering, and reliable email notifications for both clients and admin.

Key Features:

  • Mobile-first, accessible UI with optimized touch interactions
  • Online booking calendar with time-slot selection, staff assignment and rescheduling
  • Service & package pages with duration, pricing, and optional add-ons
  • Product and voucher ordering with secure checkout and order management
  • Automated email notifications and reminders (client & admin) via queue worker
  • Admin panel for managing bookings, staff, services, orders and content
  • Performance optimizations: image lazy-loading, asset minification and caching
  • Payment gateway integration and order reporting
  • SEO, analytics and accessibility improvements (WCAG basics)

Booking & Notifications:
Users can book appointments quickly on mobile or desktop, receive instant booking confirmations, and get reminder emails. Admin can review, confirm or reassign bookings from the dashboard and receives notifications for new orders and bookings.

Admin & Management:
The admin dashboard provides role-based access, calendar overview, service CRUD, order handling, reporting and simple audit logs to monitor activity.

Technologies Used: Laravel, Bootstrap, MySQL, Redis (queue/caching), AJAX, SMTP for emails, payment gateway integration.

Additional Features:

  • Promo codes, discounts and dynamic pricing for packages
  • Gallery, testimonials and blog management for SEO/content
  • Optional SMS/WhatsApp notification hooks and OTP verification
  • Privacy-conscious data handling and cookie notices
