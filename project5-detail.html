GoldenTouch Spa & Parlor — Website

goldentouchspa.com.np is a professional spa and parlor website developed with Laravel during a paid internship. The platform focuses on service bookings, staff scheduling, product/orders (e.g. spa packages and gift vouchers), and secure admin management.

Key Features:

  • Responsive, modern UI optimized for mobile and desktop
  • Online booking calendar with time-slot selection and staff assignment
  • Service & package pages with duration, pricing and add-ons
  • Secure admin panel to manage bookings, staff, services and content
  • Automated email notifications for booking confirmations, reminders, cancellations (client & admin)
  • Order management for products/vouchers with payment gateway integration
  • Customer accounts, appointment history and rescheduling
  • Contact form with validation, spam protection and notification routing
  • Analytics integration and SEO-friendly structure

Booking & Notifications:
Customers can reserve appointments, choose staff or preferred slots, and receive instant email confirmations and reminders. Admin receives notifications for new bookings and can confirm or modify appointments from the dashboard.

Admin & Management:
The admin panel enables staff management, service CRUD, calendar overview, reporting, and handling orders/vouchers. Role-based access control secures sensitive operations.

Technologies Used: Laravel, Bootstrap, MySQL, AJAX, Stripe/PayPal (or local gateway), SMTP for emails.

Additional Features:

  • Promo codes and discounts during checkout
  • Gallery and client testimonials management
  • SMS or WhatsApp notification hooks (optional integration)
  • GDPR / privacy-conscious data handling for customer data
