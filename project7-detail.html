Tours XTen — Package Booking Platform

tours.xten.com.np is a Laravel-based travel platform focused on tour package and destination bookings. The site provides a straightforward package-only booking flow and a CMS for managing packages, galleries, blogs and bookings. Email notifications are sent to both clients and admin on booking events.

Main Capabilities:

  • Package-only booking: users select package, choose date & passenger details and submit booking
  • Clear package detail pages with itinerary, inclusions/exclusions and gallery images
  • Mobile-first booking flow optimized for quick discovery and booking on devices
  • Admin CMS to manage packages, bookings, gallery images, blog posts and contact inquiries
  • Integrated email notifications for booking confirmation and admin alerts (queue-based delivery)
  • Booking management in admin: view, confirm, cancel and export bookings
  • SEO-friendly content pages and blog management for destination content

Booking & Notifications:
The booking process is intentionally simple — no cart functionality. Once a booking request is submitted the system validates availability, stores the booking and queues confirmation emails to the user and an alert to admin; admin confirms bookings from the dashboard.

Admin & Content Management:
Admin has role-protected access to a lightweight CMS for packages, blogs, galleries and contact messages, enabling fast content updates and media uploads.

Technologies Used: Laravel, Bootstrap, MySQL, AJAX, SMTP for mailing, server-side validation.
