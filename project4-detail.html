Astrological Website for Amrit Poudel

astroamritpaudel.com is a comprehensive astrological platform built with Laravel, designed for Astrologer Amrit Poudel. The website offers detailed astrological services, personalized horoscope readings, and a seamless booking system for consultations.

Key Features:

  • Modern, responsive design for all devices
  • Detailed service pages for astrology, numerology, and vastu consultations
  • Online booking system with calendar integration
  • Stone crafts and jewelry ordering with secure checkout
  • Automated email notifications for clients and owner on bookings and orders
  • Admin dashboard for managing bookings, orders, and content
  • Client testimonials and blog section for astrological insights
  • Multi-language support (Nepali & English)
  • SEO optimized for better reach

Booking & Ordering:
Users can easily book astrology sessions, select preferred dates, and receive instant email confirmations. The stone crafts/jewelry section allows clients to order custom items, with notifications sent to both the client and the astrologer for every transaction.

Email Notifications:
The system sends automated emails for booking confirmations, reminders, and order status updates, ensuring smooth communication between the astrologer and clients.

Technologies Used: Laravel, Bootstrap, MySQL, AJAX, SMTP for emails.

Additional Features:

  • Secure authentication for clients and admin
  • Dynamic content management for blogs and services
  • Integrated payment gateway for orders
  • Custom horoscope generator
  • Contact form with validation and spam protection
